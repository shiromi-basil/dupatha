Hotels in Galle

Colombo is a vibrant town in the southern province of colombo, and provides a wide selection of hotels compared due to being a tourist hotspot in the country. Being the city by the seashore the best hotels here provide an ocean veiw with most five star hotels being located in close proximity to galle fort.There are a wide variety hotels you can chose from. With such a wide variety of hotels you can be sure to find a hotel that will fit well into your budget as well. If you do not hope to stay full board also do not worry as galle is famous for its street food that can cater to your needs.
