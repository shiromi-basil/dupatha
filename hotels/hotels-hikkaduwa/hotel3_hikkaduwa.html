Hotels in Hikkaduwa

hikkaduwa is a beautiful town by the ocean world famous for its white sandy beaches. Its a gorgoeus tourist attraction in sri lanka and has several hotels to cater to the thousands of tourists who flow in every year. It is very poplar for its coral reefs and so many of its hotels lie on the beach.Hikkaduwa is a seaside resort town in southwestern Sri Lanka. Its known for its strong surf and beaches, including palm-dotted Hikkaduwa Beach, lined with restaurants and bars. The shallow waters opposite Hikkaduwa Beach shelter the Hikkaduwa National Park, which is a coral sanctuary and home to marine turtles and exotic fish. Inland, Gangarama Maha Vihara is a Buddhist temple decorated with hand-painted murals.
