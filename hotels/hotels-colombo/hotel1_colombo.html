Hotels in Colombo

Colombo is the beautiful city in the easter province of colombo, as the capitol city of Sri Lanka it provides the widest selection of hotels compared to any other place in the country. Being the city by the seashore the best hotels here provide an ocean veiw with most five star hotels being located on the infamous galle face strech, but if you came to Sri Lanka on buisness there are several comforatble city hotels you can chose from. With such a wide variety of hotels you can be sure to find a hotel that will fit well into your budget as well. If you do not hope to stay full board also do not worry as colombo has thousands of restaurants that can cater to your needs.
