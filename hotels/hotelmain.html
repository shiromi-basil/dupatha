Where to stay in Sri lanka

Haven't yet found a place to stay for when you visit sri lanka? You have nothing to worry about.You will be spoilt for choice with all the different types of hotels Sri Lanka has to offer. Whether you are looking for a relaxing holiday in the hills or an exiting adventure on the beach you will have no trouble in finding the perfect type of accomodation for you.

With hotels ranging from 1 to 5 stars you can plan for a trip well within your budget and still experience the tropical bliss this island has to offer.You can expect friendly service and the best standards in whichever range you choose. Find out more about the different types of accommodation and facilities they offer here.

  • 5 Stars

    • Superior standard and an extensive range of first-class guest services

  • 4 Stars

    • Exceptional standard with high-quality furnishings and comfort

  • 3 Stars

    • Well-appointed establishments with comfortable, clean rooms and quality furnishings

  • Less than 3 Stars

    • A basic, well-maintained standard
