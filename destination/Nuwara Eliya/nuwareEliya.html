Often referred to as ‘Little England’, this genteel highland community does have a rose-tinted, vaguely British-country-village feel to it.
Visit everyone’s favourite hill-country village. The views through Ella Gap are stunning, and on a clear night you can even spy the subtle glow of the Great Basses lighthouse on Sri Lanka’s south coast. Don’t be too laid-back though; definitely make time for easygoing walks through tea plantations to temples, waterfalls and viewpoints.

Major Attractions

Lake Gregory

Gregory Lake was created in 1873 by the Governor at the time, Sir William Gregory, for the purpose of providing electricity but it was used for recreational activities.

Lover's leap Waterfall

Lover’s leap fall, located at Hawaeliya is the closest water fall from the city of Nuwara Eliya. There is a beautiful legend behind this beautiful small cascade.

Victoria Park

This is one of the country's most attractive, and best-maintained, town parks. A stroll around its paths, past manicured lawns, is a pleasure.

Pidurutalagala

It is the tallest mountain in Sri Lanka and has a height of 2524m. The surrounding forest has many rare trees. The mountain top gives a beautiful view.

Hakgala Botanical Garden

It was established for cultivation of the medicinal crop Cinchona then, for Tea cultivation and in 1884 it was changed into a garden with tropical plants.

Sri Pada/Adam's Peak

Adam’s Peak the place where Adam first set foot on earth after being cast out of heaven) or Sri Pada Sacred Footprint, left by the Buddha as he headed towards paradise.

Nine Arches Bridge

The Nine Arch Bridge in Ella is on the Demodara loop. The beautiful nine arches make it a very picturesque spot especially as it is located in a dense jungle.

Bambarakanda Falls

It is the tallest waterfall of Sri Lanka with of height 263m. There is a natural pool complex at the base of the falls and it is a great spot to take pictures.

World's End

It is a sheer cliff, with a drop of about 4,000 feet (1,200 m). It is one of the most visited parts of the Park and it gets really misty by 10am.
